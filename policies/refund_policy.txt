Refund Policy

We aim to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued.

Eligibility for Refunds
- Customers may request a refund within 14 days of receiving the product.
- The product must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging.
- Proof of purchase is required for all refund requests.

Non-Refundable Items
- Gift cards are non-refundable.
- Downloadable or digital products are not eligible for refunds.
- Items marked as "final sale" cannot be refunded.
